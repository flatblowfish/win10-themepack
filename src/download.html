    Personalize your Windows 10 device with a wide variety of new, great looking themes from the Microsoft Store.

    Get more themes

    A theme is a combination of desktop background pictures, window colors, and sounds. To get a theme, expand one ofthe categories, click alink forthe theme, and then click Open. This saves the theme to your PC and puts it on your desktop. See Personalize your PC to learn more.
